About Hirevoa

Hirevoa is a startup platform built for young founders. It connects aspiring entrepreneurs with co-founders, team members, mentors, and the resources needed to build from scratch.

Share and validate ideas, post roles for your startup team, learn through guides and courses, and build with practical tools like AI startup mentors, idea generators, pitch deck builders, and startup dashboards.

Join challenges, demo days, and a startup launch space — while collaborating with a community that helps you move faster.

Hirevoa is a startup platform for young founders to turn ideas into real companies — with co-founder matching, team building, mentorship, guides, and practical tools.

A short-video style feed (30–60 seconds) where founders share updates about their startup journey — ideas, progress, problems, demos, and wins. Other users can like, comment, share feedback, and join the team through the reel.

Create a profile with your skills and startup interests, then discover founders and builders filtered by what you need. Founders can also post roles so people can apply to join startups.

In spirit, it’s a modern startup ecosystem — but built specifically to empower young founders with a community space, mentorship networks, guides, and builder tools that help you plan, build, and launch.
